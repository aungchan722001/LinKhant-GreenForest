About Us

Green-Forest Plants and Accessories Shop has been your trusted source for high-quality plants and accessories since 2005. Our mission is to bring nature closer to you by offering a diverse range of greenery and gardening essentials, now available online for your convenience.

How GreenForest Was Formed

Green-Forest Plants and Accessories Shop was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate horticulturists who shared a common dream: to create a one-stop destination for plant enthusiasts and gardeners. Inspired by their love for nature and a desire to make high-quality plants and gardening accessories accessible to everyone, they opened their first shop, which quickly grew into a beloved local business known for its exceptional products and customer service.
